Registering your move on the United States Moving Protection website can provide you with several benefits and protections. First and foremost, it allows you to verify that the moving company you have selected is registered and licensed with the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA). This helps protect you from fraudulent or unlicensed movers. Additionally, by registering your move, you can access helpful resources, such as routes, tips on how to plan and prepare for your move, as well as information on your rights and responsibilities as a consumer. Finally, registering your move can help you resolve any disputes or claims that may arise during the moving process.
